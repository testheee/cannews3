The most popular professions in the next 10-20 years (with the highest salaries)

New technologies are changing our lives beyond recognition. Today's life is very different from what we all lived 10-20 years ago.And what awaits us and our children/grandchildren in 10-20 years?What kind will professions be the most popular (and most highly paid)? This is exactly what we will talk about in this article.This is the most exciting time for the growth of our economy and the growth of our income. Because today every second person of working age can become a millionaire (and remain anonymous in the labor market). At the same time, a person's income will become significantly higher if he is prepared to invest and work for a long time.The main thing here is to decide on the right one — to become a millionaire or not. To do this, you need to make a few preliminary preparations:1) Start saving money.To do this, get into the habit of regularly spending 10-20% of each of your income on the "financial cushion". 2) Start investing.As a rule, in the long run, investments generate higher returns than buying individual assets. 3) Start getting rich.This is the easiest way — get rid of your debts and get rich.It all depends on your priorities and goals.What will you do to do this?What will you spend your remaining money on?The more you prepare for this, the more money you will be able to create over the horizon:After you have formed a "financial cushion" of sufficient size, transfer it to investments.Make a good habit of buying currency, stocks, and other financial instruments every month.This will allow you to gradually build substantial capital (the income from which will fully provide you and your entire family). 1) Read my article " Where NOT to invest money?
