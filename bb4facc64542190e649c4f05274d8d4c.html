A powerful factor for personal income growth

"They say that happiness is in the head." Just like money.So, too, it makes sense. Or so-called "collective unconscious," or "collective unconscious" — this is a person's attitude to life after the death of the main source of income.In this article, we will talk about how each person can create their own "financial cushion" and find prosperity. 1) Chronic lack of money.This is the most insidious of all "cones" that "attract" money to your wallet.Many people have a hard time of it be they've never had enough money before, or they can't afford to buy the money they earn. This is the main obstacle that stands between you and a lot of money. 2) Pay yourself every time you spend money.Never do this.
